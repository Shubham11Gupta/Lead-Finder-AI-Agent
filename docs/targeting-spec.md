# Targeting Spec

## Problem we solve
 - The Gap : D2C consumable brands struggle to convert first-time buyers because shoppers hesitate to buy full-size products without trying.
 - Consumer Friction : Shoppers do not fully trust paid reviews and avoid full-size purchases without low-risk trials.
 - Brand Burn : CAC on Meta and Google is rising while trial-to-full-size conversion remains weak.
 - Data Gap : Existing sampling flows are often transactional and do not produce structured, actionable consumer intelligence.

## Who we help (ICP)
 - Industry: D2C consumables (beauty/personal care, nutrition, snacks, pet care, health care)
 - Company size: 1(solo) to 60 employees
 - Stage: Pre-seed to Series A, including pre-revenue and unregistered startups
 - Region: India
 - Current motion: Founder/Growth-led online sales with active or emerging performance marketing on Meta/Google

## Signals of need
 - Recently launched a product/SKU and needs early users fast
 - Running paid social ads but low review depth/UGC
 - Frequent first-order discounts (conversion friction signal)
 - Public request for reviews/testimonials on social channels
 - Operates in repeat-purchase consumable categories
 - Early-stage startup with newly active digital presence (new website/social/store listings)
 - Already experimenting with samples/trial packs

## Buyer persona inside ICP
- Founder / Co-founder
- Head of Growth / Performance Marketing
- E-commerce Manager / Brand Manager

## Disqualifiers
- Non-consumable categories where sampling is impractical
- Very low-margin products where sample economics do not work
- Enterprise brands with mature in-house trial programs

## Offer + CTA
 - Offer: "We help D2C brands run a closed-loop sample journey: users claim a Trio Box, submit verified feedback, earn Trio Coins, and convert to full-size purchase while brands receive structured zero-party insights."
 - CTA: "Would you be open to a 15-minute pilot discussion for one SKU and a measurable trial-to-full-size conversion goal?"
